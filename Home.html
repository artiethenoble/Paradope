ABOUT US

Paradope is a multimedia collective which aims to cultivate the Hip Hop culture in a positive way, enrich the creative urban culture and make a positive impact on the community. It's dedicated to growing the creative urban scene by making spaces where creative individuals can share their creativity and by providing up coming artists with a platform to showcase their art forms.

This is carried through events and various other media initiatives which promote creative freedom and attract creative individuals who love the urban culture to get together, collaborate and have a good time.

Paradope is for creatives who ambitiously follow their dreams, lovers of the urban creative culture
who live the culture who live to be dope
who reject the norm and choose to be in a parallel universe
